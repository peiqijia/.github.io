About Me

Hi! My name is Peiqi.

I am a Chinese girl who studies in graphic design major, currently attending the University of Massachuesetts Dartmouth. For most of my life, I have lived in my hometown Zhengzhou, China. I started to learn drawing since four years old. Then my life connected with art.
When I grow up, I started to write Chinese Calligraphy, it is another kind of art which can make me concentrate on my work. Later, I decided come to American to study the graphic design, because I want to learn more skills about design. Also, I like creating differnt kind of interesting stuff. Today, I am a designer, Calligrapher, and Photographer.
In my free time, I enjoy writing Chinese Calligraphy,shooting some beautiful photos, listening to music, hand drawing the things I like, and going on as many adventures as I can with friends and family.
